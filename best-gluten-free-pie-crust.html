Best Gluten Free Pie Crust, According to a Taste Test

The best gluten free pie crust shouldn’t taste gluten free. It should be flaky, delicate, and buttery, like a normal, gluten-full pie crust!

We tasted five different ready-made gluten free pie crusts and were actually impressed with three. In fact, these three gluten free pie crusts were so good that I’m now contemplating making a gluten free pie this holiday season. Whether you abstain from gluten or not, you will love the best gluten free pie crust! Note: The scoring on these is not an error—these are all 10/10 Spork gluten free pie crusts. They’re that good.

Best GF Graham

Mi-Del Graham Cracker Pie CrustWalmart

Mi-Del knows what’s up. They make some of the best gluten free pie crust options out there. I’m not sure how they have a handle on gluten free products in a way that no one else does, but it’s just the truth. This graham cracker crust is one of the best gluten free pie crust versions we had. This tasted so similar to regular graham cracker pie crust that I think you’d be hard-pressed to find the difference once you made a pie with it. Do not hesitate to buy this store bought gluten free pie crust. It rocks.

Best GF Chocolate

Mi-Del ChocolateWalmart

I would not know this was gluten free if I hadn’t read the packaging. It’s so tasty! It is the best gluten free pie crust because it tastes really similar to the Oreo pie crust we all know and love. This would be such an easy swap if you were entertaining gluten free guests over the holiday season. A chocolate pie in this Mi-Del crust would be a gluten free dessert dream come true.

Credit: Merc / Walmart

Best of the Best

Wholly Wholesome Gluten Free Pie CrustInstacart

If you’re looking for a gluten free version of a standard pie crust, buy this frozen gluten free pie crust from Wholly Wholesome! This pie crust is crunchy, sugary, and light. I cannot think of a sweet filling that wouldn’t thrive stuffed into one of these gluten free pie crusts. Having this on hand is going to make your holiday season one thousand times easier, more convenient, and more inclusive! Just think about how touched your friend or family member will be when you make a totally gluten free dessert for them to enjoy with this gluten free frozen pie crust.
