Shiwani Saini is a high-spirited actress, born on 31st December 1988, hailing from India and has worked in Hindi, Punjabi, and English movies. She made her debut movie with her Punjabi movie “Go Lucky”. She has done graduation in Fashion Designing.  During studies, she took part in Lakme Fashion Week which was held in 2012. Afterward, she worked with a Theater Group “Alankar” with Yuva Puraskar winner -Chakresh Kumar.

Career

Besides acting, she is a well-trained martial art and loves riding bikes. Since ten years, she has been riding Royal Enfield Bullet which she has learned from her brother. Before joining the industry, she worked in an export house as a merchandiser.

Initially, she took the start of her career with the Punjabi film industry, and later, she moved into Bollywood and then, Hollywood film industry where she worked in numerous movies. When she was 28 years old, she first appeared in a Punjabi debut movie “Go Lucky” which was produced by Harry Bawaeja which was released in 2014. While it was produced by Baweja Productions and directed by Amarpreet G.S & Chabra. In 2016, Shiwani Saini appeared in the movie “Sarabjit” which was directed by Omung Kumar. Also, she has worked in 5 Indo-American comic movies for which she got huge acclaim.

Moreover, she has also worked as a model for many notable brands including Shudh Gold. Also, she has appeared in the short movie “Dhee Da Phela Pyar” which was about the theme of cancer awareness.

Achievements

Shiwani Saini has got huge recognition and fame during her career life and still hoping to gain more success in the future.
