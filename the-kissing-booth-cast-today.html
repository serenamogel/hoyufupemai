The Kissing Booth Cast Today: Where Are They Now?

The films follow the love story of Elle Evans (Joey King) and Noah Flynn (Jacob Elordi), as well as their

friendship with Noah’s younger brother Lee Flynn (Joel Courtney).

But what are the actors behind these characters doing now? Are they still in touch with each other?

And are Noah and Elle still together in real life? Here’s what we know about the current status of the Kissing Booth cast.
