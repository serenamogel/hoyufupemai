Who is David Malpass? David Malpass Biography and Wiki

David Malpass is an American economic analyst who is recognized as the President of the World Bank Group since 4th April 2019. He is also a notable former government official serving under Donald Trump’s administration as the Under Secretary of the Treasury for International Affairs.

David Malpass Education

Malpass is an alumnus of Colorado College where he received his B.A. in physics. Before that, he attended the University of Denver and earned his MBA. After Colorado, the economic analyst studied international economics at Georgetown University’s School of Foreign Service.

In addition, Malpass speaks Spanish, Russian, and French. He got his first official job in 1977 working for Esco Corporation and Arthur Andersen’s systems consulting group in Portland, Oregon. While working there, he became a licensed CPA.
