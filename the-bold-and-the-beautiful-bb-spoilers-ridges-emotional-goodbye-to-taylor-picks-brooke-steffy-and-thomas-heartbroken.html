Ridges Emotional Goodbye To Taylor, Picks Brooke Steffy And Thomas Heartbroken

The Bold and the Beautiful (B&B) spoilers tease that Ridge Forrester (Thorsten Kaye) will finally decide who he wants to marry. Brooke Logan (Katherine Kelly Lang) urged Ridge to do some soul-searching to end the cycle of going back and forth between her and Taylor Hayes (Krista Allen).

Brooke tells Ridge she wants a life with him, but he needs to look within himself to discover what he really wants. If he wants her, he must close the door on Taylor completely. And she added that if he selects Taylor, he needs to let her move on and never come back to her.

The Bold And The Beautiful Spoilers– Ridge Forrester Decides Between Taylor  Hayes And Brooke Logan

It’s been years of going back and forth between Taylor and Brooke. B&B spoilers reveal that both women are sick of it and want Ridge to make a decision and stick with it. Now that Ridge is back this week (December 12), the Bold and the Beautiful fans will finally learn who Ridge selected.

Taylor has wanted to be Ridge’s wife for many years. She longs for her family to come together and enjoy life with Ridge and their children, Steffy Forrester (Jacqueline MacInnes Wood) and Thomas Forrester (Matthew Atkinson).

The B&B spoilers for the week reveal that Ridge chooses a woman, and the viewers think he will pick Brooke. It’s an educated guess based on showrunner Brad Bell’s obsession with making Brooke Ridge’s “destiny.”

The Bold And The Beautiful Updates – Ridge Forrester’s Choice Breaks Steffy Forrester And Thomas Forrester’s Hearts

Let’s assume Ridge picks Brooke. If that happens, it will devastate Steffy and Thomas. Their dreams of their parents uniting will be over, and Taylor will struggle to accept Ridge’s decision.

Thomas will likely blame Steffy for Ridge’s decision. He will say that if she had stayed quiet, their parents would be on their honeymoon right now. Thomas isn’t wrong, but Steffy did the right thing. Ridge deserved to know the truth.

Ridge’s decision will likely upset Taylor, Steffy, and Thomas. Brooke will know that Ridge loves her and wants a life with her, turning his back (yet again) on Taylor and his children.
