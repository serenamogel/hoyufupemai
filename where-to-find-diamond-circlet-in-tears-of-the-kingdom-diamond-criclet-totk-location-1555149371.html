What is Diamond Circlet?

The Diamond Circlet is an elegant headgear featured in several installments of the Legend of Zelda game series, including Breath of the Wild, Age of Calamity, and Tears of the Kingdom.

This opulent headpiece is adorned with a diamond that harnesses the power of light, effectively reducing the damage inflicted by monsters, particularly Guardians. In Tears of the Kingdom, the Diamond Circlet serves a unique purpose of augmenting the wearer's attack power. It stands as an individual armor, distinct from any matching set, and while it can be upgraded, dyeing it is not an available option.

In Breath of the Wild, adventurers can uncover the Diamond Circlet within a treasure chest located in a chamber beneath Lomei Labyrinth Island. Conversely, in Tears of the Kingdom, it is obtainable through purchase at Starlight Memories, the jewelry store situated in Gerudo Town, for the cost of 2,400 Rupees.

It is essential to note that access to Starlight Memories hinges upon the completion of the Main Quest. Thus, diligent players should undertake the necessary quests to access this coveted piece of headgear.

Where to Find Diamond Circlet in Tears of the Kingdom?

To obtain the Diamond Circlet in Tears of the Kingdom, you must venture to Gerudo Town and follow these steps:

  • Head to Gerudo Town: The first step is to make your way to Gerudo Town, the specific location where the Diamond Circlet can be found.
  • Visit the Jewellery Shop: Once in Gerudo Town, seek out the Jewelry Shop known as "Starlight Memories."
  • Purchase the Diamond Circlet: At Starlight Memories, you can acquire the Diamond Circlet for the price of 2,400 Rupees.
  • Complete the Main Quest: Prior to gaining access to Starlight Memories, it is vital to complete the Main Quest as a prerequisite.
