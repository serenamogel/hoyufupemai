“No Cap No Kizzy” is an expression commonly used in hip-hop culture. In this context, “No Cap” means no lies or no exaggeration, while “No Kizzy” translates to “no kidding” or “for real.” Together, these phrases emphasize the sincerity and authenticity of the song’s message. Young Jexf utilizes this jargon to convey his genuine emotions, experiences, and thoughts.

The lyrics of “No Cap No Kizzy” explore the journey of Young Jexf’s rise to fame, the challenges he faced along the way, and his unwavering determination to succeed.
