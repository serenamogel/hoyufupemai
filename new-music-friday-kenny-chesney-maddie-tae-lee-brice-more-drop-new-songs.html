Kenny Chesney, Maddie &amp; Tae, Lee Brice &amp; More Drop New Songs Celeb Secrets Country

TGIF!

Some of our favorite country artists released some hot new tracks, albums and music videos this week and we can’t get enough of them.

This week’s New Music Friday features fresh music from an array of talent, including a new album from Maddie & Tae; new songs from Kenny Chesney, Mitchell Tenpenny, Lee Brice and Tenille Townes; and new music videos from Luke Combs, Dustin Lynch, Tucker Beathard, among others. It’s literally music to our ears!

Keep scrolling to see which artists dropped new tunes and let us know what you think of them by either leaving a reaction at the bottom of the post or by sending us a tweet at @CS_Country.
